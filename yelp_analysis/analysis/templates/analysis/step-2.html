Problem :

If a person is willing to establish new business based on similar business categories available in Yelp Database, The major problem is that which categories of that particular business a person should put together to allure more number of customers and earn handsome amount of good ratings.
(E.g. If a person want to start a restaurant business then what kind of cuisines should a person put together to make more profit ? )

Solution :

The solution to the above problem is to find out relevant business data from yelp database and then applying an apriori algorithm to group similar categories and by setting up appropriate support and confidence which will give us association rules.
